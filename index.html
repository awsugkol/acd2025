WHO ARE WE?

AWS User Group Kolkata (AWSUGKOL) is a Kolkata based group united by community-led learnings and diversified by the wide variety of tools and technologies closely knit with Amazon Web Services, cloud computing technology, AWS design implementation and servicing, high scalability / performance computing, production use cases of AWS, blockchain, Artificial Intelligence and much more.

Join us on 5th April 2025 for the much awaited AWS Community Day Kolkata 2025, unveiling success stories, use cases & much more from AWS and industry leaders.

AWS Community Days are community-organized cloud education events, featuring technical discussions and demos led by expert AWS users and industry leaders from around the world

Community-based learning is the best way to enhance the concepts being taught by connecting with enthusiasts in person, thereby sharing first-hand experiences and familiar, accessible examples.

All skills levels are welcome to help accomplish our motto of learning, sharing and networking. Let's join hands to empower this tech community to make it bigger and better!

AWS Powering India's Tech Revolution


Amazon Web Services (AWS) is playing a pivotal role in driving India's technological transformation. As the country embraces digitalization, AWS provides a robust cloud infrastructure that supports startups, enterprises, and government initiatives. With its vast array of services, including computing power, storage, and machine learning, AWS enables businesses to innovate rapidly and scale efficiently. India's burgeoning tech ecosystem benefits from AWS's global reach and local data centers, ensuring low-latency access and compliance with regulatory requirements. The platform empowers developers and entrepreneurs to build cutting-edge applications, harness big data, and leverage artificial intelligence, fostering a culture of innovation.
