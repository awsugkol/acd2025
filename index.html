WHO ARE WE?

AWS User Group Kolkata (AWSUGKOL) is a Kolkata based group united by community-led learnings and diversified by the wide variety of tools and technologies closely knit with Amazon Web Services, cloud computing technology, AWS design implementation and servicing, high scalability / performance computing, production use cases of AWS, blockchain, Artificial Intelligence and much more.

Join us on 5th April 2025 for the much awaited AWS Community Day Kolkata 2025, unveiling success stories, use cases & much more from AWS and industry leaders.

AWS Community Days are community-organized cloud education events, featuring technical discussions and demos led by expert AWS users and industry leaders from around the world

Community-based learning is the best way to enhance the concepts being taught by connecting with enthusiasts in person, thereby sharing first-hand experiences and familiar, accessible examples.

All skills levels are welcome to help accomplish our motto of learning, sharing and networking. Let's join hands to empower this tech community to make it bigger and better!

HONORABLE GUEST


Sanjay Kumar Das

Additional Secretary, Science & Technology & Bio Technology Dept & State Information Security Officer, Government of West Bengal, India

Welcome Keynote


Sanjay Kumar Das is a seasoned public administrator with over 27 years of experience, currently serving as Additional Secretary in the Science & Technology & Bio-Technology Department of the Government of West Bengal. He has spearheaded significant initiatives in digital transformation, including the KarmoBhumi platform, which connects IT professionals with local firms, and the Self-Scan mobile application, enhancing digital privacy. A key figure in West Bengal's cybersecurity landscape, he established the state's Cyber Security Centre of Excellence and has contributed to embedding cybersecurity education in polytechnic curriculum. His accolades include the DSCI Security Leader of the Year Award 2024 and multiple national awards for his contributions to governance and cybersecurity. With a background as an Intelligence Officer and advanced degrees in law and business, he is also a published poet and co-founder of UDDYOM, an NGO focused on education and poverty alleviation, continuing to influence technology-driven policy and administration in the region.


TECHNICAL KEYNOTE SPEAKER


Dr. Biswajit Mohapatra

Head, Customer Solutions, AWS India and South Asia

AWS Powering India's Tech Revolution


Amazon Web Services (AWS) is playing a pivotal role in driving India's technological transformation. As the country embraces digitalization, AWS provides a robust cloud infrastructure that supports startups, enterprises, and government initiatives. With its vast array of services, including computing power, storage, and machine learning, AWS enables businesses to innovate rapidly and scale efficiently. India's burgeoning tech ecosystem benefits from AWS's global reach and local data centers, ensuring low-latency access and compliance with regulatory requirements. The platform empowers developers and entrepreneurs to build cutting-edge applications, harness big data, and leverage artificial intelligence, fostering a culture of innovation.


COMMUNITY KEYNOTE SPEAKER


Aditi Sawhney

Data-Driven Marketing Leader at AWS

Bridging the Gap: How AI Helps Tech Leaders Tell Better Stories


"Bridging the Gap: How AI Helps Tech Leaders Tell Better Stories" explores how artificial intelligence enhances storytelling in the tech sector by enabling data-driven insights, personalizing narratives for specific audiences, and streamlining content generation. AI tools facilitate effective visual storytelling and predictive analytics, allowing leaders to craft compelling narratives that resonate with stakeholders. Additionally, AI aids in real-time feedback analysis, enabling continuous improvement of messaging and helping to simplify complex concepts for better communication between technical and non-technical audiences. Overall, AI empowers tech leaders to communicate more effectively and engage their audiences with clarity and impact.
