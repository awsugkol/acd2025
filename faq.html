Frequently Asked Questions

ASK QUESTION?

Who should attend AWS Community Day Kolkata?

There are many reasons. It is Global but local AWS Community event. Please refer our agenda and goal of AWS Kolkata community.

What is your cancellation policy?

Registration charges are non refundable and non transferable.

What is included in event registration?

Registration gives you access to the keynote, all breakout sessions and closing reception. Meals offered include lunch and snacks. You are also eligible for the goodies, but make sure you stay till the end of the event to entitled for this. you can not claim it untill the event is concluded.

Can I stop by onsite without being registered?

No! Due to the high demand for AWS Community Day Kolkata, we do not grant access to walk-ins with no upfront registration at the event venue. Thank you for your understanding!

Where is my confirmation email?

If you have registered, you should receive a confirmation email and SMS shortly after registering. If you do not receive a confirmation email, please check your spam folder. Once you have checked your spam folder, if you still haven't received your confirmation email, please contact AWS User Group Kolkata.

Will the sessions be in English/Hindi/Gujarati language?

All sessions will be in English since our audience is coming from across the states or country and we want to make sure everybody can follow.

Do you have any travel policy for the speakers?

No! we do not provide travel assistance to the speaker. Speaker has to born the travel to speak at the event by himself.

How can I contact the event organizers?

If you have questions that have not been answered in the FAQ above, please contact AWS User Group Kolkata.
